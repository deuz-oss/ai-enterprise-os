# AEP-048 — AI Software Factory Autonomous Improvement & Self-Optimization Standards

**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)
**Status:** Reference Standard

---

# 1. Purpose

Dokumen ini menetapkan standar untuk kemampuan autonomous improvement, optimization, dan continuous evolution pada AI Software Factory.

Tujuan utama:

* Membuat platform mampu mengidentifikasi peluang improvement.
* Mengoptimalkan proses engineering secara otomatis.
* Meningkatkan kualitas AI Agent, workflow, dan platform.
* Mengurangi ketergantungan pada optimasi manual.
* Membangun self-improving engineering system.

---

# 2. Core Principles

Self-improvement mengikuti prinsip:

* Human Controlled Autonomy.
* Evidence Based Improvement.
* Safe Experimentation.
* Continuous Optimization.
* Measurable Impact.
* Reversible Change.

AI boleh mengusulkan dan melakukan improvement, tetapi tetap berada dalam governance boundary.

# 7. Autonomous Improvement Levels

---

## Level 0 — Manual

Manusia melakukan semua improvement.

---

## Level 1 — Assisted

AI memberikan rekomendasi.

---

## Level 2 — Supervised Automation

AI melakukan perubahan dengan approval.

---

## Level 3 — Controlled Autonomy

AI dapat melakukan improvement terbatas otomatis.

---

## Level 4 — Adaptive System

AI mampu mengoptimalkan dirinya berdasarkan feedback.

---

## Level 5 — Self-Evolving Platform

Platform mampu menemukan dan menerapkan evolusi besar dengan governance manusia.

# 8. Improvement Proposal Standard

Setiap improvement harus memiliki:

* Problem statement.
* Current state.
* Proposed change.
* Expected benefit.
* Risk analysis.
* Validation method.
* Rollback plan.

# 9. AI Engineering Optimization

* Approval workflow.

* Rollback mechanism.

---

# 14. Optimization Metrics

Improvement diukur berdasarkan:

* Productivity gain.
* Quality improvement.
* Cost reduction.
* Reliability improvement.
* User satisfaction.

---

# 15. AI Improvement Governance

Setiap autonomous action harus:

* tercatat,
* dapat dijelaskan,
* dapat dihentikan,
* dapat dikembalikan.

---

# 16. Human Role

Manusia berperan sebagai:

* Goal setter.
* Constraint designer.
* Final authority.
* Strategic decision maker.

AI berperan sebagai:

* Analyst.
* Experimenter.
* Optimizer.
* Assistant.

---

# 17. Review Checklist

Sebelum improvement otomatis diterapkan:

* Evidence tersedia.
* Impact dihitung.
* Risk diperiksa.
* Test selesai.
* Rollback tersedia.

# 20. Compliance

Tidak ada autonomous improvement yang boleh mengubah production system tanpa mengikuti governance, audit, dan approval mechanism sesuai AEP-043 dan AEP-042.
