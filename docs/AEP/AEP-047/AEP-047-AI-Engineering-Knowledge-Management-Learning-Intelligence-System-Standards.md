# AEP-047 — AI Engineering Knowledge Management & Learning Intelligence System Standards

**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)  
**Status:** Reference Standard

---

# 1. Purpose

Dokumen ini menetapkan standar pengelolaan knowledge, organizational learning, dan intelligence system untuk AI Software Factory.

Tujuan utama:

* Menangkap seluruh engineering knowledge.
* Membuat knowledge dapat digunakan kembali.
* Memungkinkan AI belajar dari pengalaman organisasi.
* Mengurangi pengulangan kesalahan.
* Membangun institutional intelligence.

AI Software Factory harus menjadi sistem yang semakin pintar setiap kali digunakan.

---

# 2. Knowledge Principles

Knowledge system mengikuti prinsip:

* Capture Everything Valuable.
* Reuse Before Reinvent.
* Context Matters.
* Knowledge Has Ownership.
* Continuously Updated.
* Machine Readable.
* Human Understandable.

# 4. Knowledge Domains

Knowledge dibagi menjadi:

---

## Architecture Knowledge

Berisi:

* Architecture Decision Record.
* Design pattern.
* Trade-off.
* System topology.

---

## Engineering Knowledge

Berisi:

* Coding standard.
* Implementation pattern.
* Debugging solution.
* Best practice.

---

## AI Knowledge

Berisi:

* Prompt pattern.
* Agent behavior.
* Model evaluation.
* AI experiment.

---

## Business Knowledge

Berisi:

* Process.
* Requirement.
* Domain rule.
* Business decision.

---

## Operational Knowledge

Berisi:

* Incident.
* Recovery procedure.
* Performance optimization.

# 8. AI Knowledge Retrieval

AI Agent dapat:

* mencari solusi sebelumnya,
* memahami konteks proyek,
* menemukan pattern,
* memberikan rekomendasi.

---

# 9. Knowledge Quality Management

Setiap knowledge memiliki:

* Owner.
* Version.
* Confidence level.
* Last reviewed.
* Usage count.
* Validation status.

---

# 10. Learning From Projects

Setiap proyek menghasilkan:

* Architecture learning.
* Code learning.
* Process learning.
* AI learning.

Selesai proyek bukan akhir knowledge.

---

# 11. Incident Learning System

Setiap incident menghasilkan:

* Root cause.
* Resolution.
* Prevention.
* Updated knowledge.

Incident menjadi aset pembelajaran.

---

# 12. AI Assisted Knowledge Creation

AI dapat membantu:

* membuat dokumentasi,
* merangkum diskusi,
* menemukan pattern,
* membuat tutorial,
* memperbarui knowledge.

---

# 13. Knowledge Governance

Setiap knowledge harus memiliki:

* Owner.
* Reviewer.
* Expiration policy.
* Access policy.

Knowledge yang salah harus dapat diperbaiki.

# 15. Knowledge Metrics

Diukur berdasarkan:

* Search success rate.
* Reuse frequency.
* Knowledge freshness.
* Resolution improvement.
* Time saved.

---

# 16. AI Agent Learning Rules

AI Agent wajib:

* menggunakan knowledge resmi,
* memberikan referensi sumber,
* menyimpan insight baru,
* tidak menganggap output sebagai fakta tanpa validasi.

---

# 17. Knowledge Security

Kontrol:

* Access control.
* Classification.
* Data privacy.
* Sensitive knowledge protection.

---

# 18. Review Checklist

Pastikan:

* Knowledge memiliki owner.
* Metadata lengkap.
* Mudah ditemukan.
* Validasi tersedia.
* Dapat digunakan AI.

# 20. Long-Term Vision

Target jangka panjang:

AI Software Factory mampu:

* mengingat seluruh pengalaman organisasi,
* belajar dari keberhasilan,
* belajar dari kegagalan,
* meningkatkan keputusan engineering,
* menjadi semakin pintar dari waktu ke waktu.

AI Software Factory bukan hanya factory yang membuat software.

AI Software Factory menjadi **learning organization**.

---

# 21. Compliance

Seluruh implementasi enterprise AI Software Factory harus memiliki knowledge management capability sesuai AEP-047.
